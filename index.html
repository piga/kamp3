About Romantica autocamp


Small family camp


Started in 1979, the first camp in that location. Everything started my father, who is working in tourism all his life. So we have big experience working with tourists.

The camp is located in beautifull small bay. There are also other autocampings in that bay. We have only olives trees in the camp, so there in not much shadow, unfortunately. Zone A is closer to the sea and has more shadow that zone B, which is at the top of the camp, but zone B has beautifull views on the sea and islands.

We have about 35 positions for guests. You can come with tents, campers, boats, ... we have place. We always have some free places, so you don't have to worry that there will not be place for you. But if you are planing to come than it would be nice that you mail or call us one week before comming so we can prepere for you.

In SERVICES section you can see more informations about power, water, ...

We live in the camp so you will never be alone. You will always have our help.
